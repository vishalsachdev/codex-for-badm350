# Spring 2026 Featured Case Studies

These are BADM 350 student team case studies that the instructor selected as standout examples from Spring 2026. Each was originally submitted to the full class collection at [`badm350-spring-2026-case-studies`](https://github.com/vishalsachdev/badm350-spring-2026-case-studies); the strongest 3–5 are mirrored here as teaching material for future cohorts working through Module 3.

## Why these are here

Future students working through Module 3.2 ("Build Your Automations") can read these to see what *good* looks like — real automations built by their peers on real semester projects, with honest reflection on what worked and what didn't.

## Opt-in

Students whose case studies appear here explicitly opted in for public inclusion in the course repo. Submissions that stayed in the class-only repo are equally valid; they just weren't opted in for reuse.

## Format

Each file follows the template at the class repo: [`_TEMPLATE.md`](https://github.com/vishalsachdev/badm350-spring-2026-case-studies/blob/main/_TEMPLATE.md).
