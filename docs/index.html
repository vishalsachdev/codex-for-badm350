FAQ

Common questions.

Do I need to know how to code?

No. Zero coding required. The entire course is designed for people who have never written a line of code. You'll direct AI using plain English — the same way you'd brief a contractor or manage a team.

What exactly is Codex?

Codex is an AI coding agent made by OpenAI. It's a free desktop app for Mac and Windows where you open a project folder and have a conversation. You describe what you want in plain English, and Codex builds it. No coding required. It works with a free ChatGPT account — no paid subscription or API keys needed.

How long does the course take?

About 5–6 hours total, split across four modules. Modules 0–2 (~4.5 hrs) teach you Codex through NovaBrew. Module 3 (~1.5 hrs) applies it to your own project. Go at your own pace.

What do I get at the end?

Two deployed automation artifacts for your semester project (one marketing, one operations), plus a before/after reflection you can use in your Week 15 board memo. And everything you built in Modules 0–2 (NovaBrew app, GitHub portfolio).

Do I need my semester project ready?

Yes. Module 3 requires your BADM 350 project — live URL, Checkpoint 4 report, and knowledge of your product. Modules 0–2 use NovaBrew, so you can start those anytime.

Is this a team or individual assignment?

Modules 0–2 are individual (learn the tool). Module 3 is team-based — your team picks 2 automations together and builds them for your shared project.
